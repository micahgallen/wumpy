================================================================================
                            ROOM SUMMARY
================================================================================

MAIN STREET (North to South):
1. teleport_booth        - Portal to other realms (Florida, Texas, etc.)
2. sesame_street_north   - Northern terminus with bench
3. sesame_street_03      - Mid-north section, hotel access
4. sesame_street_01      - CENTRAL PLAZA (hub) with Big Bird & Oscar
5. sesame_street_02      - Mid-south section with hopscotch
6. sesame_street_south   - Southern entrance/exit to Reality Street

BUILDINGS (East/West branches):
7. general_store         - Hooper's shop (east from central plaza)
8. bar                   - Furry Arms Tavern (west from central plaza)
9. hotel_lobby           - Snuggly Sleeper Hotel (west from mid-north)
10. hotel_room_01        - Guest room (west from lobby)

COMBAT ARENA (South from bar):
11. arena_lounge         - Pit Fighter's Lounge (preparation area)
12. arena_main           - Wumpie Colosseum (combat testing ground)

TOTAL ROOMS: 12

================================================================================
                            DESIGN NOTES
================================================================================

THEME: Satirical 90s nostalgia meets Terry Pratchett wit
TONE: Friendly but slightly off, wholesome with subtle darkness
STYLE: Tongue-in-cheek, observational humor, unexpected details

WUMPY MECHANICS:
- Low-level (Level 1) creatures
- Kickable for player amusement
- Each has distinct personality
- Roaming property set for wandering behavior

CONNECTIONS TO OTHER REALMS:
- North: Teleport booth → Other realms (not yet implemented)
- South: Reality Street → Darker area (not yet implemented)

EXPANSION OPPORTUNITIES:
- Additional hotel rooms
- Back rooms for bar/store
- Alleyways off main street
- Basement/secret areas
- More interactive NPCs
